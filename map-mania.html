Getting Started

Rules of the game: Use pan and zoom to search for marked locations on the map.

You must be within a close enough zoom level of a location for it to appear.

Once you find a location, start searching for the next one, use hints if stuck.

To automatically win the game, press the "Win Now" button.

Click Close Modal button to close instructions, or click anywhere outside of the modal.
